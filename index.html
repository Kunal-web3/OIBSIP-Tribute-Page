Inspiring Generations

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam (1931–2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. Popularly called the "Missile Man," he was instrumental in India's space and defense programs, and inspired millions of youth through his humility, vision, and dedication to education.
